Auto Spareparts

The leading provider of quality motor spareparts in Uganda

Who are we?

We are an e-commerce company specialising in procuring spare parts for all types of vehicles on demand.

What do we do?

We provide any spare parts you may need for your car on demand. All you need to do is contact us with a short description of what you're looking for and we will procure it and deliver it to you typically within 5 business days.

Please Note that some deliveries may take longer if the part you need is not currently within our stock.

More about us:

Our vision is to one day encompass Africa with our convenient door to door delivery services such that vehicle owners need not suffer while procuring hard to find spare parts ever again.

Above all, we value accuracy and speed.
Although we deliver fast, we place even more emphasis on identifying exactly what you need and will never deliver the wrong product. To ensure this, we confirm your order as soon as we locate a matching product.
