Auto Spareparts

The leading provider of quality motor spareparts in Uganda

What do we provide

We locate and deliver spare parts for any vehicle in the shortest possible timeframe. We source these parts from suppliers within the country but we also import them if there are none available locally.

How can you start the process?

Reach out to any of our personnel or send a message from our Contacts Page and we can get the ball rolling.

We will typically require you to identify the make and model of your vehicle and the type of spare part you are looking for. All this will be done via email, or in person at our offices.

Please Note that some deliveries may take longer if the part you need is not currently within our stock.
